Meet Juls

The science of healing meets the wisdom of the body.

As a Doctor of Public Health (DrPH) and RYT-200 yoga teacher, Julianna bridges the gap between evidence-based wellness and embodied healing.

Based in Atlanta, she creates space for LGBTQIA+ and BIPOC communities to land, breathe, and return to themselves through trauma-informed care.
